LEADERSHIP STYLES

DIRECTIVE

Workplace Characteristics

Unstructured interesting tasks,clear formal authority and good group cohesion.

Employee Characteristics

inexperienced employees,they believe that they lack power and expect leader to direct them.

SUPPORTIVE

Workplace Characteristics

Simpler more predictable tasks,unclear or weak formal authority and poor group cohesion.

Employee Characteristics

Experienced confident employees,they believe that they have power and they reject lose control.

PARTICIPATIVE

Workplace Characteristics

Unstructured complex tasks,formal authority could be either clear or unclear and group cohesion could be good or poor.

Employee Characteristics

Experienced confident employees,they believe that they have power and they reject lose control,preferring to excercise power over their work.

ACHIEVEMENT ORIENTED

Workplace Characteristics

Unstructured complex or unpredictable tasks,clear formal authority and group cohesion could be good or poor.

Employee Characteristics

Experienced confident employees,they believe that they lack power and they accept the idea of the leader setting their goals and have a lot of respect for the leader.
